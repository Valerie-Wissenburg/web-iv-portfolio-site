Identity System and Integrated Branding

Francesca’s
2015

Francesca’s is a boutique for women in their twenties that love higher priced clothes. These women are sophisticated, elegant and like unique clothes. I chose to handletter the logo because Francesca’s like to make it known that every piece of clothing is one of a kind just like they believe their company is one of a kind.
