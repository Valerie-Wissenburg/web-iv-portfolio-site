Single Barrel: Informational Booklet
2015

This is a promotional booklet for Single Barrel because they’ve decided to make their restaurant/venue high quality and fancy. Their food is high quality and they are starting to match the sophistication. They were looking to attract the richer 21-30 year olds. Colors help relate and bring the elegance back to the ground for more of a comfortable presence.
