Annual Report

Chipotle Mexican Grill
2015

This client was looking for an exciting annual report to tell shareholders how they were doing this past year while maintaining their branding standards. These shareholder enthusiasts are looking for something that’s easy to understand and energetic. The annual report shows personality with bold tasteful colors that help the reader not be bored. The handlettering comments on the spreads ties the company’s personality into the annual report. I chose to handletter certain elements because Chipotle is playful and has a hand in the good ethics from where there produce comes from.
