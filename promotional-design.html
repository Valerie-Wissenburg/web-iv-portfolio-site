Promotional Design

Cooking Class: Sponsored by Whole Foods
2015

Whole Foods has a cooking class that happens every thrid wednsday of the month. This promotional design showcases the events through different elements. Whole Foods is targeting unexperienced or bad cookers in their mid-twenties. They are looking for an exciting and hopeful way to advertise the cooking class.
