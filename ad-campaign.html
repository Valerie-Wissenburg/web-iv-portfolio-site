Copic Markers Dual Tip
2015

Adult creatives between the ages of 25 - 35 that want to use a marker that is simple and excellent to use. This group of adults are free spirited, outgoing, and love to color with markers. Copic markers want to show that the two tipped markers are dual personalities. By showing this, I decided to do prey and predator. Both are polar opposites but come together in nature. It's natural to add fragile personailty to a strong one. It creates a balance that's needed in everything.
