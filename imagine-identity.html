Imagine Identity System

Imagine: Build your own ice cream shop
2015

Imagine is a build-your-own ice cream shop for young women on a budget. They are modern and contemporary than the old time ice cream shoppes. Imagine wanted an appealing, minimal logo that represented them. It's a build-your-own shop, so it was only fitting to hand draw the logo because Imagine wanted to show the interaction the customers have with their produce through the logo.
