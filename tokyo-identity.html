Identity System

Tokyo Japanese Steakhouse and Sushi Bar
2015

Tokyo is a Japanese and Sushi restaurant. That has a vibe to be family friendly, minimal and modern. Their customers are willing to pay for good asian cuisine at a higher price. Tokyo is looking for a logo to represent themselves as a contemporary, higher class Japanese restaurant. Ultimately, Tokyo is looking for a sleek, geometrical feel. I chose to handletter the name because they are active in working with their hands and involved with their customers' good experiences.
