Paint Yourself Silly is looking for a new playful website. They want to direct it towards the young adult that might have children or looking for information for themselves. They don’t want something ridiculous because the company is still professional and a friendly user experience and interesting for preteens and up. Paint Yourself Silly wants a bright and memorable site to keep for as long as possible.
